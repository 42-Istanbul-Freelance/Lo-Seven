# Project Proposal: PearlConnect — The Social Impact Network

## 1. Executive Summary
PearlConnect is an innovative platform that reimagines traditional volunteer tracking by merging it with modern social media dynamics. It serves as a verified ledger for social responsibility efforts while providing an engaging, hybrid experience of LinkedIn and Instagram. The platform allows users to share stories, post media, and build professional volunteer portfolios, ensuring both high engagement and strict institutional oversight.

## 2. Core Objectives
* **Dynamic Tracking:** Regularly track social responsibility activities within an engaging digital environment.
* **Verified Recording:** Systematically record volunteer hours to create a verified record of impact.
* **Institutional Benchmarking:** Measure performance and impact on a school-by-school basis.
* **National Analytics:** Generate a nationwide impact analysis for the General Headquarters.
* **Digital Portfolio:** Produce a verified digital volunteering document for use in university and job applications.

## 3. The Social-Institutional Hybrid Model

### A. User Roles & Hierarchical Access
The system utilizes a multi-layered permission structure to ensure proper oversight:
* **Students:** Individual accounts for logging activities and creating social content.
* **Teachers:** Coordinating accounts that oversee a specific group of students, with the power to approve or reject activity logs.
* **School Principals:** Institutional accounts that manage the school's overall profile and oversee the teaching staff.
* **General HQ:** Administrative access for nationwide reporting and data analysis.

### B. Social Networking Features
* **Interactive Feed:** Users can create posts (images, videos, reels) showcasing their volunteer work.
* **Story System:** Temporary 24-hour visual updates for real-time engagement.
* **Engagement Tools:** Features to like, comment on, and share posts to individual stories.
* **Follow Mechanism:** Accounts can follow one another, and students can officially link their profiles to verified school institutions.

## 4. Volunteer Management Workflow

### A. Activity Logging & Verification
* **Submission:** Students log activities (Seminars, Stands, Donations, etc.) including date, duration, and description.
* **Evidence:** Mandatory photo or document uploads serve as proof for every logged activity.
* **Approval Flow:** Teachers utilize a dedicated panel to review submissions, request edits, or finalize approval.
* **Auto-Calculation:** The system automatically tracks total, monthly, and yearly hours against goals (e.g., 30-40 hour targets).

### B. Gamification & Digital Rewards
Verified hours unlock badges visible on the user's public profile:
* **Bronze Pearl:** 25 hours.
* **Silver Pearl:** 50 hours.
* **Gold Pearl:** 100 hours.
* **Platinum Pearl Leader:** 200+ hours.
* **Institutional Awards:** "Pearl Friendly School" and "Social Impact Leader" badges for top-performing institutions.

## 5. Technical Stack & Security

### A. Proposed Technologies
* **Backend:** Spring Boot (Java) for secure, scalable API development and complex role-based logic.
* **Database:** PostgreSQL or MySQL to manage the intricate relationships between students, teachers, and institutions.
* **Frontend:** React or Next.js for a responsive, mobile-first social experience.
* **Storage:** Cloud-based storage (e.g., AWS S3) for secure media and document hosting.

### B. Security & Data Privacy (KVKK)
* **Encryption:** Student data must be encrypted and stored securely.
* **Verification:** Media uploads are subject to institutional approval.
* **Parental Consent:** Digital permission workflows for minor users.

## 6. Reporting & HQ Dashboard
The General Headquarters can generate the following outputs:
* City-based impact maps and total national volunteer hours.
* Top 10 most active students and schools.
* Performance distribution charts based on activity types.
